GST Reconcillation

The GST Reconciliation tool helps match sales and buy data from various returns and sales and purchase gisters. The tool entails comparing two sets of data entries to find any discrepancies or deviations. It is done to fix any unintended mistakes or omissions from before.     If you receive thousands of invoices per month, even a single-digit percentile will represent a sizeable volume. As a result, under GST, the data from the returns must be reconciled regularly.

Asset Management

With the Fixed Asset Management System (FAMS), you can devise the easiest path to develop, operate, maintain and sell assets in a way that is both economical and efficient. In this manner, your stakeholders would be aware of the resources that can be used to provide the best results. With FAMS, you can rule out these concerns and manage your assets in the most effective way.

Data Room

A cloud storage software for securely storing confidential information, while simultaneously analysing and sharing it. A housing space for data of secure and privileged nature, data rooms include data storage, document exchange, file sharing, financial/legal transactions and more. You can manage data virtually with personalized tools and screens, accompanied by complete ownership and custody of your data.
